AS A... STUDENT


Over the course of two graduations you can learn a variety of things


Gabriel has been studying technology since 2013. It all started in Brazil, his homecountry, when he started a graduation in System's Analysis and Development, at Instituto Federal da Bahia. This program was focused totally in programming, giving Gabriel the skills to learn and develop software related to web, desktop and database. Some concepts of networks, distributed systems and sotware engineering were also learned. After that, Gabriel went to Canada, where he was enrolled at Computer Software and Database Development at Lambton College of Technology, Science and Applied Arts. This program made him get deeper in his studies, providing him advanced concepts of database, mobile development and further Java and C# development, graduating from the program with a GPA of 3.93

AS A... RESEARCHER


Experience of almost two years as a researcher


Gabriel has worked as a researcher in both colleges he studied during his academical career. The topics of those researches were in different areas, giving him opportunity to know and develop new skills and studies. While a researcher from IFBA, Gabriel was able to work with distributed systems, specifically at the Self-Adaptive Software area, developing a new approach for transition between top-down and bottom-up states. At Lambton College, however, the focus was on Quality Assurance processes, giving him perspectives on how to improve assembly processes on technology companies.

AS AN... ANALYST


When being an analyst at Ericsson, everything is about practice


Since mid 2018, Gabriel has been working at Ericsson Inovacao, in the branch of Salvador/Brazil. His role there has the goal to evolve an automation framework, using Ruby and Cucumber, from which automated tests are executed. As part of the Quality Assurance team, some manual tests are also executed around the Online Charging System that the company takes care. This is proving to be an amazing experience, in spite of the challenge of dealing with the busiest real-time online system of Latin-America. This environment of a big company also provides knowledge about some cutting-edge practices used at the IT market.
